CV

I was born in Athens, Greece, in 1977. I hold a 5 year Diploma in Chemical Engineering from NTUA since 2001. I also hold a MSc in Computational Mechanics from NTUA since 2003. I hold a PhD in Operational Research from NTUA since 2010.

I have worked as a postdoctoral researcher at AUEB Department of Statistics, Academy of Athens Research Center for Astronomy and Applied Mathematics, and AUEB Department of International and European Economic Studies.

I have taught classes at Panteion University, National and Kapodistrian University of Athens and AUEB.
